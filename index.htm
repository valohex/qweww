FAQ

Are these shaders easy to install and how do I remove them?

Yes! Simply install them by placing the files in your Roblox directory. To remove them, delete the shader files.

Will these shaders affect my FPS?

Yes, shaders can lower your FPS, especially on low-end systems. Make sure you meet the requirements.

Are these shaders safe to use?

Yes, they are safe as long as you download them from this site.

Can I use these shaders on mobile?

No, these shaders are designed for PC and require a dedicated GPU.

Do I need a high-end PC to run shaders?

Not necessarily, but having a powerful GPU will ensure better performance.

Do these shaders work on all versions of Roblox?

Most shaders work on the latest version of Roblox, but updates may affect compatibility.

Can I customize the shader settings?
